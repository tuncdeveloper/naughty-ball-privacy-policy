Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

This privacy policy applies to our mobile ball game and explains how we handle user data.

1. Information We Collect

Our game collects the following information:

  • Game scores and progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Crash reports (to improve the app)
  • General usage statistics
We DO NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone number).

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is only used for:

  • Improving the game experience
  • Fixing technical issues
  • Analyzing game performance

3. Information Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. Anonymous statistics are only used for game development purposes.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. The game features family-friendly content and an educational level progression system. We provide a safe gaming environment for children.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information. Most data is stored locally on your device.

6. Your Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • All local data is deleted when you uninstall the game
  • We can delete collected data upon request
  • You can stop data collection at any time

7. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes.
