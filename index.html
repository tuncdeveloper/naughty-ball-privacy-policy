Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

This privacy policy applies to our mobile ball game and explains how we handle user data.

1. Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect any personal information from users of any age.

Our game stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game scores and level progress
  • Game settings and sound preferences
  • Level completion status

We DO NOT collect, transmit, or access:

  • Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or error logs
  • Network activity or internet browsing data
  • Camera, microphone, or contact list access
  • Any data transmitted to external servers or third parties

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This app is designed for children aged 3 and above and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

For Children Under 13:

  • We do NOT knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13
  • We do NOT require registration, account creation, or login
  • We do NOT use tracking technologies, cookies, or analytics
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • All game data is stored locally on the device only
  • No internet connection is required to play the game

Parental Rights:

  • Parents can delete all local game data by uninstalling the app
  • Parents have full control over their child's data since it remains on their device
  • No parental consent is required as we collect no personal information

If we discover we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete such information.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data usage. All game progress and settings remain on your device for gameplay purposes only.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. Your gameplay remains completely private on your device.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. We cannot access your game data as it never leaves your device.

6. Your Rights

  • All local data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the game
  • You have complete control over your local game data
  • No data collection occurs that requires user consent

7. Third-Party Services

This game does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. The game operates completely offline.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
